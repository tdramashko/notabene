About Us

Independent expertise.
Practical results.

Notabene was founded to provide high-quality, independent audit and tax services to businesses in Cyprus and internationally.

Our team comprises qualified accountants and auditors who are members of ACCA and ICPAC. We hold practising certificates for audit and assurance services.

We combine technical expertise with a practical, client-focused approach — whether you are a local SME, an international group, or a company navigating the regulatory landscape.
